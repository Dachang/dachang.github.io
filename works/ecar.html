ECAR

Electric Car Rental and Charging Service

What are the benefits ECAR provides



When compared with traditional car rental services, electric car rental stands out as it has been proved to be more effective. Electric car rental meets the needs of short distance travel and daily commute perfectly, meanwhile it provides a broader network distribution than tradition car rental business.


More importantly, electric car rental reduces the cost of private use of vehicles, makes up the gap for public transport, reduces the ownership of motor vehicles, mitigates issues from energy consuming to air pollution, as urban environment protection becomes increasingly eventful for citizens. We expect the service itself ultimately becomes an essential unit of the sustainable development of the city.

Find or Charge Electric Cars Nearby

All electric cars rental and charging points displayed in detail, choose the one closest to you. Pick up your car at designated time.

Track Your Rental Record

ECAR constantly saves your electric car rental history, as well as everytime you charge your car. A good rental record increase the chances earning offers and extra discounts.

Review Car Conditions before You Rent

Get to know every detail of the car you are going to rent. Select the model you like, review every related characteristics such as mileages and so on.

Service Distribution

Time-Sharing

Rent car by hours. Reserve for any time durantion you want & pick up your car beforehand.

Long-Term

Rent car by days, or in even longer time scale on lease if you are an enterprise user.

Pile Rental

Got you own electric car? You still can use any of our charging piles in network.
