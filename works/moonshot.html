WHAT IS MOONSHOT PROJECT



This project aims to explore the framework and mechanism of future car services in terms of third-party applications. We made a showreel film as the final output, it is produced based on well-designed storyboards to demonstrate those most ideal using scenarios. The infotainment system is the core of the design process as well as the main section throughout the film.


Before started designing the infotainment system, we confirmed the major scenes that jointed the showreel film. This was followed by the produciton of storyboards, which basically pinned down the functional elements of the infotainment system.


It was essential to keep the integration and consistency of the infotainment system while designing various 3rd party applications. After adopting visual frameworks, it was suprisingly cheerful to see how different apps eventually been put together.
