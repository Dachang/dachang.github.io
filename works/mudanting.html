MU DAN TING

An Experimental Live Performance of Modern Dance

Intro

‘Mu Dan Ting’ is an experimental live performance which applies technology on modern dance. We developed the multimedia interactive visual with Microsoft Kinect, by using which we captured the body gestures and movement of dancers, then we fetched the depth graph image and the skeleton data, filled it with our visual content, then export the final visual effect through projectors to the audiences.

Hanging and connecting curtains onsite.

Dancers are rehearsing in between gigs.

Body gestures of dancers are captured and processed by programming.

Lightings and facilities onsite.

The show was presented during the 2014 Shanghai International Fine Art Festival, which was held at Shanghai Theatre Academy.
